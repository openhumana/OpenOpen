About Open Humana

Your Digital Employee Agency — bridging the gap between human talent and AI-powered efficiency to transform outbound sales forever.

Our Vision

Open Humana was founded on a simple but powerful idea: every business deserves access to silicon-based teammates that never sleep, never quit, and never underperform. We believe that business owners should focus on strategy, relationships, and growth — while Digital Employees handle the grunt work.

We envision a future where hiring an AI-powered employee is as natural as hiring a human one — except faster, cheaper, and infinitely more scalable. Our Digital Employees are built to augment your team, not replace it, giving you the leverage to compete with companies ten times your size.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make world-class AI employees accessible to every business, regardless of size or budget. Whether you're a solo entrepreneur or a growing sales organization, Open Humana gives you the same caliber of digital workforce that was previously only available to enterprise-level companies.

We're democratizing access to AI-powered outbound sales, personalized voicemail drops, live call transfers, and intelligent campaign management — all wrapped in a simple, intuitive platform that anyone can use.

Our Values

Innovation

We push the boundaries of what AI can do for sales teams, constantly improving and iterating on our technology to stay ahead of the curve.

Reliability

Your Digital Employee works 24/7/365 with perfect consistency. No sick days, no off days, no excuses — just dependable performance around the clock.

Transparency

Clear pricing, honest communication, and full visibility into every call, voicemail, and result your Digital Employee produces.

Scalability

From one Digital Employee to an entire agency, our platform grows with your business — scale up or down instantly without long-term commitments.

Simplicity

Powerful technology shouldn't be complicated. We design every feature to be intuitive, so you can launch campaigns in minutes — not hours.
