Help Center

Everything you need to know about your Digital Employees

What is a Digital Employee?
A high-performance AI agent trained on your specific business data. Your Digital Employee — like Alex — handles outbound calls, drops personalized voicemails, transfers live calls to your team, and works 24/7 without breaks. Think of it as a tireless teammate with superpowers who never calls in sick.
How much does it cost?
$99/mo salary per employee plus pay-as-you-go usage fees for calls and voicemails. There are no contracts, no hidden fees, and no minimum commitments. You can scale up or down at any time. Compare that to a $4,000+/mo human BDR with benefits, training costs, and turnover risk.
Can I fire a Digital Employee?
Yes, instantly, with no HR paperwork, no severance, and no awkward conversations. Cancel your plan anytime and your Digital Employee is deactivated immediately. No questions asked. You can always rehire later if you change your mind.
How do I get started?
Sign up, choose your plan, and Alex will be ready in 10 minutes. Upload your contact list, configure your voicemail preferences, set your transfer number, and hit launch. It's that simple — no technical setup, no integrations required, and no training period needed.
What languages does Alex speak?
Alex speaks 50+ languages fluently, including English, Spanish, French, German, Mandarin, Japanese, Portuguese, Arabic, Hindi, and many more. Each voicemail and interaction is delivered with native-level pronunciation and natural intonation, so your prospects feel like they're talking to a local.
How does live call transfer work?
When a human picks up, you get instantly notified and the call is transferred to your team in real-time. You'll receive a push notification and/or SMS alert with the prospect's details so you're fully prepared before you even say hello. Never miss a hot lead again.
