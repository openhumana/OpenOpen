Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information

Last updated: February 2026

Information We Collect

When you use Open Humana's services, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, company name, and billing details provided during registration.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our platform, including call logs, campaign data, feature usage, and session duration.
  • Contact Lists: Phone numbers, names, and other contact details you upload for use with our dialing and voicemail services.
  • Communication Data: Call recordings, voicemail transcripts, and any messages exchanged through our platform.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information, and cookies used to improve your experience.

How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our Digital Employee services
  • Process transactions and send related information, including confirmations and invoices
  • Personalize your experience and deliver content relevant to your interests
  • Send technical notices, updates, security alerts, and support messages
  • Monitor and analyze trends, usage, and activities to improve our platform
  • Detect, investigate, and prevent fraudulent transactions and other illegal activities

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information. This includes encryption of data in transit and at rest, regular security audits, access controls, and secure data storage practices. While no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data.

All call recordings and voicemail data are encrypted using AES-256 encryption. Access to customer data is restricted to authorized personnel on a need-to-know basis.

Third-Party Sharing

We take your privacy seriously. Here is our commitment regarding third-party data sharing:

  • We do NOT sell your lead data. Your contact lists, call data, and prospect information are yours and will never be sold to third parties.
  • We may share data with trusted service providers who assist us in operating our platform (e.g., cloud hosting, payment processing, telephony providers), subject to strict confidentiality agreements.
  • We may disclose information if required by law, regulation, or legal process.
  • In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction, with prior notice provided to you.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. Call recordings and campaign data are retained for a period of 12 months unless you request earlier deletion. After account termination, we will delete or anonymize your data within 90 days, unless retention is required by law.

You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting our support team.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data, subject to certain legal exceptions.
  • Portability: Request a copy of your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Opt-Out: Opt out of marketing communications at any time.
  • Restriction: Request that we restrict the processing of your data under certain circumstances.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information below.
